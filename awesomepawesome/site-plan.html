Purpose

You ever heard of the saying, dogs are men's best friends? We couldn't agree more with that statement, as we treat our doggy companions as more than just friends, but as family. Here at Awesome Pawesome, we aim to give provide your dogs with the best possible products on the market, for the best price! Whether it is kibbles, food bowls, toys or even clothes to dress up and spoil your dog! We have products suitable for dogs of all sizes! From the smallest Chihuahua, to the biggest Great Dane, we include all in our dog family!

APPAREL AND GROOMING

APPAREL (LEASH)Need a new leash for your strong and growing dog? We have the perfect leash for you! Whether you need a strong leash for a dog that pulls, or an extendable leash to let your puppy explore the world, we have everything you need! (HARNESS) If you are afraid your leash is choking your dog, use one of our harnesses instead! Our harness are long lasting and comfortable, perfect tool for a nice chill walk in the park with your best friend! (FUN CLOTHES) Attending a party with your pooch? Don’t leave him out of the fun! We have a wide range of apparel, suitable for any occasion! Whether it’s a Halloween party, a birthday party, or even a wedding! We have the perfect suit for your best friend! GROOMING (COMB/BRUSH) Have a shaggy dog that sheds a lot? Do not worry, we provide stainless steel brushes. Whether your dog has straight fur, curly fur, long fur or matted fur, we have the perfect brush for the perfect grooming! (CLIPPERS)Long nails? Struggling pup? Do not worry, our clippers are long lasting and will clip your pets long nails so fast they will not even notice it! (SOAP/SHAMPOO)Is your dog starting to smell? We have the best shampoos on the market, whether you need anti fleas or anti dandruff, or just a nice smelling shampoo, we have the product to satisfy your pets’ needs. (PERFUME)No time for a bath? No worries! We have the best smelling perfumes that are pet-friendly. It will counter your dog’s odour in an instant! (EARWIPES) Does your dog scratch its ear non-stop? We’re here to stop that! Purchase our effective and safe ear wipes, to get rid of that nasty gunk causing your pet’s ear to itch. (General Wipes) Our pet wipes will ensure that you can get rid of unwanted floor stains while being safe for the pets to sniff. This is because our pet wipes do not contain any harmful chemicals as we care very much for your pets well being.

FOOD AND TOYS

FOOD (KIBBLES)Our food products are 100% pet-safe and if your pet has any specific needs such as having pre-existing medical conditions such as allergies to certain types of food, we have the solution. From salmon and venison kibbles. If you need a cheaper yet still healthy kibble, we have chicken, pork and even beef kibbles to satisfy your hungry puppy! (SNACKS) Our snacks are considered to be very high value by many trainers as most pups or full grown dogs love our snacks! You can use it for training, teaching or even just giving your good ol’ friend a nice treat for fun! TOYS (BONES) IS your dog in its teething phase?
